About

I'm also expanding my skills in Next.js, Webflow, and enterprise-level scalability to create products ready for the future. Right now, I live in Hyderabad, and beyond coding, you'll find me exploring ideas for indie projects, writing about tech experiences, or planning my next big adventure. ❤
